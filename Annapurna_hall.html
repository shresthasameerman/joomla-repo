About Annapurna Hall

Annapurna Hall at White Leaf Resort is an epitome of elegance and sophistication. Designed to host a variety of events including weddings, corporate functions, and social gatherings, this hall stands out for its spacious layout and versatile interior. The hall can comfortably accommodate a large number of guests, making it perfect for grand celebrations.

The hall is equipped with modern amenities including high-quality sound systems, ambient lighting, and customizable seating arrangements. The decor features a harmonious blend of natural tones, creating a tranquil and stylish environment that enhances the overall experience of any event.
