About Everest Hall

Everest Hall at White Leaf Resort is the pinnacle of luxury and grandeur. Perfect for hosting high-profile events such as corporate meetings, banquets, and lavish weddings, Everest Hall is designed to leave a lasting impression. The expansive space and opulent decor make it an ideal choice for those looking to make their event truly special.

The hall is outfitted with top-of-the-line amenities including cutting-edge sound systems, dynamic lighting options, and adaptable seating configurations. The interior decor features a sophisticated palette of green, white, and black, providing a chic and serene setting that enhances the elegance of any event.
