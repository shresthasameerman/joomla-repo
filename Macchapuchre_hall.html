About Macchapuchre Hall

Macchapuchre Hall at White Leaf Resort is known for its majestic ambiance and state-of-the-art facilities. Ideal for hosting a variety of events such as conferences, gala dinners, and wedding receptions, this hall is designed to impress. The spacious interior and elegant decor make it a top choice for any grand occasion.

The hall offers an array of modern amenities including advanced audio-visual equipment, customizable lighting, and flexible seating arrangements. The decor features a sophisticated blend of green, white, and black, creating a serene and stylish atmosphere that enhances the prestige of any event.
